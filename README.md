# Cotidiana Memoria – Privacy Policy

*Last updated: February 28, 2026*

## Your Data Stays Yours

Cotidiana Memoria is designed with privacy at its core. All your schedules, assignments, and study data are stored locally on your device. Nothing is collected, tracked, or shared with anyone.

## iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. This data is managed entirely by Apple under their own [privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We have no access to your iCloud data.

## No Analytics or Tracking

Cotidiana Memoria does not use any analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or any other telemetry.

## No Third-Party Services

The app does not communicate with any third-party servers. There are no external API calls, no data sharing, and no remote processing of your information.
