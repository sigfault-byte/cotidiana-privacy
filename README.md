# Cotidiana Memoria – Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 28, 2026

## Your Data Stays Yours

Cotidiana Memoria is designed with privacy at its core. All schedules, assignments, and study data are stored locally on your device. Nothing is collected, tracked, or shared.

## iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple’s CloudKit. This data is managed by Apple under their own privacy policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

## No Analytics or Tracking

Cotidiana Memoria does not use analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not collect usage data, telemetry, or analytics.

## No Third-Party Services

The app does not communicate with external servers controlled by us. There are no external API calls, no data sharing, and no remote processing of your information.
